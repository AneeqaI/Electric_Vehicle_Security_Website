FAQs:

What is EV Security and why does it matter?

EV Security focuses on protecting electric vehicles (EVs) from digital threats like hacking, data breaches, and remote access vulnerabilities. As EVs become smarter, securing their software and data is more important than ever.

What kind of stories can I share on this platform?

You can share anything from security-related incidents, suspicious behavior, or scams to helpful tips and lessons learned from EV ownership. Your experience might help someone else!

Will my submission be reviewed or moderated?

Currently, all stories are submitted instantly to the community feed. However, we may implement moderation tools if needed to keep the space safe and respectful.

Do I need an account to share my story?

Nope! Just fill out the form and submit it! We keep things simple and privacy-friendly.
