Our story

EV Security Hub started with a simple idea of wanting to give ev users a space to share their story. Initiated by a community of safety and technology enthusiasts, we saw the growing threats and wished to form a community that could stay one step ahead. Today, we’re proud to be a trusted source for updates, tips, and insights at the intersection of electric vehicles and cybersecurity.

Our aim

Our website is designed to be a way for all EV users to have a place to read, and learn from other users experiences. Our purpose at EV Security Hub is simple: creating a knowledge hub for people to remain up to date on current news and things that happen in the world of tech and EV security. As the EV world keeps growing at breakneck speed, so do the threats — from hacking to system vulnerabilities that fly right under the noses of the most regarded and trusted manufacturers. That's why we built this site. To bring you the most up-to-date info, best practices, and in-the-trenches news on EV security in a format that's easy to understand, even if you're not a tech guru.
